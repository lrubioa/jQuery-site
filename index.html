Welcome to Lavender Resort, the place of your dreams. Set in Puerto Peñasco, we offer a wide variety of fun activities, along with being just near the main city. Enjoy parasailing, the banana boat, or even take the whole family on a cruise and watch the sea animals, like dolphins and whales!

The resort also offers a restauraunt, gift shop, and reception services downstairs. Not just that, but a pool and a hot tub for relaxing in after being in the ocean. The beach is a few minutes walk from your condo, so what are you waiting for?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my reservation for a full refund?

We accept cancellations up to 7 days before the reserved day(s) for a full refund. Anything after 7 days is not subject to a refund.

What is allowed in the resort?

We allow most things (excluding objects with possible safety and wellbeing concerns) into our condos. By the pool, we ask that guests keep track of their belongings. We are not responsible for any lost items. The beach is public, so guests can bring anything.

Can I reserve over 5 condos?

We do allow guests to reserve more than 5 condos, but we ask that they will have to contact our front desk for further information about availability and possible accommodations.

Who can I contact for other questions?

Our reception desk is available to provide additional guidance everyday from 6 am to 7 pm.
